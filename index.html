About Us

In 2012, we began to cooperate comprehensively in the field of pets. After six years of training and learning, we have accumulated a wealth of pet knowledge and gradually occupied a place in the pet market. In 2017, we cooperated with kk-pet to develop "PETX" in the PET market and ranked first in its PET products market.

Over the years, in line with the constant pursuit of "seeking knowledge, professionalism and excellence", we have won the unanimous recognition of our pet friends and won the trust and welcome of our pet friends.

In 2018, Durham House of Pets is officially opened to pet lovers, providing them with integrated services such as pet foster care, pet supplies, pet training, pet and pet funeral, so that you can keep Pets happily, have fun and travel at ease after work.
